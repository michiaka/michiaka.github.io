Event schedule checker

This app helps you find Meetup events that fit into your schedule without conflicting with your Google Calendar.

About This App

We use the Meetup API to provide users with up-to-date event information. The data is fetched responsibly and used only to display events that fit your availability.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This application accesses your Google Calendar only to check for scheduling conflicts. No data is stored, shared, or sold.

Terms of Use

By using this app, you agree to use it for personal purposes only. We comply with Meetup's API Terms of Service and ensure responsible use of their data.
